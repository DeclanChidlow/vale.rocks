Services

I host a range of services from both a computer at my house and various servers around the interweb. The current and historical statuses of all my services are displayed here. My locally hosted services include:

Fediverse

Website | Status

I host my own personal Fediverse instance. I use Akkoma which allows me to easily federate with a range of other fediverse platforms. I don't post a lot but when I do post it's usually about something techy. I'm able to follow as @vale@fedi.vale.rocks.

Jellyfin

Website | Status

My Jellyfin instance hosts a range of films, books, and television shows. The entire interface has a custom theme designed by myself. My Jellyfin is invite only.

Minetest

Website | Status

My Minetest server is a survival SMP running Mineclone 2, a Minecraft-inspired game. It offers a virtual world where players can explore, build, and interact. The server has many mods and endless possibilities, it provides a sandbox experience for creative and collaborative gameplay in a voxel-based environment. Get in contact if you are interested in joining.
